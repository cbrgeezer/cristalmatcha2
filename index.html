From Uji to Norwich

At Cristal Matcha, we bridge two worlds - the ancient tea gardens of Uji, Japan and the vibrant culture of Norwich, Norfolk. Our partnership with fifth-generation tea farmers ensures that every batch meets the highest standards of Japanese tea ceremony.

Located in the historic Royal Arcade, our Norwich boutique brings the authentic matcha experience to East Anglia.
